Harvesting

Traditional and modern rice harvest

Harvesting can be done with a sickle or with a combine harvester. Both methods gather grain efficiently when the crop is at the right maturity.

Traditional harvesting

Tool used: Sickle

Procedure:

  • Hold rice stalks.
  • Cut using sickle.
  • Tie bundles.
  • Dry harvested rice.

Modern harvesting

Using a combine harvester

Combine harvesters cut, thresh, clean and collect grain in a single pass. They are ideal for larger rice fields and faster harvests.

Machine functions

  • Cutting.
  • Threshing.
  • Cleaning grain.
  • Collecting grain.
