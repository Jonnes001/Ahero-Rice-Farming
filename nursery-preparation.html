Nursery Preparation

Preparing the rice nursery

A healthy rice nursery is crucial for strong seedlings. The Ahero process starts with fertile land, clean beds, appropriate flooding and constant moisture.

  1. Select fertile land.
  2. Clear bushes and weeds.
  3. Dig and level nursery bed.
  4. Add manure.
  5. Flood nursery lightly.
  6. Sow pre-germinated seeds.
  7. Cover lightly with soil.
  8. Maintain moisture.
