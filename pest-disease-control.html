Pest & Disease Control

Protecting rice from pests and disease

Effective scouting and control keep rice healthy. Ahero uses sound crop protection to manage common pests and prevent disease outbreaks.

Stem borers

Symptoms: dead hearts and whiteheads. Prevention includes clean fields and early scouting; control uses recommended insecticides.

Rice bugs

Symptoms: discolored grains and empty kernels. Prevent by removing alternative hosts and spray if populations build up.

Armyworms

Symptoms: chewed leaves and defoliation. Scouting helps detect outbreaks early; control includes targeted pesticide use.

Rice diseases

Common diseases and their management

Understanding symptoms and prevention helps farmers preserve rice yields and reduce losses in Ahero.

Rice blast

Symptoms: lesions on leaves and neck rot. Prevent with resistant varieties, balanced nutrition, and fungicide spray.

Brown spot

Symptoms: brown spots on leaves and grains. Control through good field sanitation, proper fertilization, and disease-resistant varieties.

Bacterial leaf blight

Symptoms: wilting and yellowing leaves. Use clean seed, remove infected plants, and apply approved bactericides if needed.
